ABOUT US

Founded in 2014 as an innovative company specialized in Design and CGI industry with head office located in Ho Chi Minh City, Fresh House has evolved promtly to establish a pretigious brand in this industry. Our services vary from high quality Design to Architectural Rendering, Animation, Virtual Reality and 3D Flythrough.

Our Vision

“One of the top 10 Design & CGI companies in Vietnam with high competitive capacity in the globe.“

Our Mission

“Never stop enhancing our qualification, maintaining our professionalism to provide the best services to customers.“

Anh Nguyen
General Director

Founder of Fresh House. He is an architect and a visualizer. He graduated from Ho Chi Minh City University of Architecture and has worked for many well-known architectural firms that gave him an extensive experience as an architect, visualizer, functional supervisor. He has discovered the power of CGI industry and became passionate about it. Since then, he has developed his CG skills to a professional level. He has won numerous admirable competitions, such as Cgvertex, 3D artist No.76, Evermotion, Treddi.com, CG 3D world.

With a strong passion for design and enthusiasm for developing the domestic 3D design industry up to the international level, Anh Nguyen has gathered a group of young and dynamic people with full of similar passion who graduated from School of Architecture throughout the country and founded Fresh House since then.

Toan Nguyen
R&D Manager

As R&D Manager, he is responsible for Business Development and Marketing Management for Fresh House in Canada and in Vietnam. He has extensive international experience in Research & Development, having lived, studied and worked for Royal Canadian Mint Instrumental in the past decade as he took part in a crucial process of making the RCM become the most innovative Mint in the world.

Thirteen years of experience in Research and Development has helped him introduce various innovations into the market. He is proud to be the owner of several successful product campaigns, numerous pioneered innovations in the worldwide market and 2 product patents.

Tin Nguyen
Investor

Tin Nguyen has graduated from a University in Melbourne, Australia. He is now taking over as CEO of Trung Thuy Corporation after he has proven himself to be very successful in many start ups. He has since then invested in numerous other businesses and Fresh House is proud to be one of the chosen ones.

After a period of exploration and working together with Fresh House, Tin Nguyen has decided to invest in Fresh House and become a share holder. With his valuable experience and expertise, he has helped to bring Fresh House to the next level of becoming one of the best 3D Archiectural Visualization Companies in South East Asia Region.

Hiep Pham
Administrative Manager

Graduated from Ho Chi Minh City University of Architecture with plenty of years of experience in design and 3D visualization, she, who is currently working as Administrative Manager at Fresh House, has been implying all her knowledge and experience to support Fresh House from the very first day.
